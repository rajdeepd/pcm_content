## Naming Conventions

1. **Concept files:** `01-concept-topic-name.md`
2. **Application exercises:** `10-concept-application-exercise-N.md`
3. **Section files:** `sectionX-description.md`
4. **Solutions:** `filename-solutions.md`
5. **Images:** `topic-description-figN.png`

## Image Extraction Strategy

1. Extract diagrams from pages
2. Name images descriptively
3. Store in `images/` subdirectory
4. Reference in markdown as: `<img src="images/eye-structure.png" width="50%">`
5. Create separate images for:
   - Eye structure diagrams
   - Defects of vision ray diagrams
   - Prism refraction and dispersion
   - Atmospheric refraction
   - Scattering diagrams
   - Colour addition diagrams
