To be extracted and compiled from all theory pages:

2. **Electric Field**: E = F/q
3. **Electric Potential**: V = W/q
4. **Ohm's Law**: V = IR
5. **Resistance**: R = ρl/A
6. **Series Resistors**: Rs = R₁ + R₂ + ...
7. **Parallel Resistors**: 1/Rp = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...
8. **Power**: P = VI = I²R = V²/R
9. **Energy**: E = Pt = VIt
10. **Wheatstone Bridge**: R₁/R₂ = R₃/R₄

## Constants and Units (For Reference Section)

### Physical Constants
- Permittivity of free space: ε₀ = 8.854 × 10⁻¹² C²N⁻¹m⁻²
- Coulomb constant: k = 9 × 10⁹ Nm²/C²

- Current: Ampere (A)
- Voltage: Volt (V)
- Resistance: Ohm (Ω)
