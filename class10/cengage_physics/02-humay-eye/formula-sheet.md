## Power of Lens

> **TODO:** Extract power formula

$$P = \frac{1}{f}$$

Where:
- $P$ = power (in diopters, D)
- $f$ = focal length (in meters)

**Sign Convention:**
- Convex lens: $P$ is positive (+)
- Concave lens: $P$ is negative (−)

## Prism Formulas

> **TODO:** Extract from prism section

### Angle of Deviation

$$\delta = i + e - A$$

Where:
- $\delta$ = angle of deviation
- $i$ = angle of incidence
- $e$ = angle of emergence
- $A$ = angle of prism

### Refractive Index (at minimum deviation)

$$\mu = \frac{\sin\left(\frac{A + \delta_m}{2}\right)}{\sin\left(\frac{A}{2}\right)}$$

Where:
- $\mu$ = refractive index
- $\delta_m$ = angle of minimum deviation
