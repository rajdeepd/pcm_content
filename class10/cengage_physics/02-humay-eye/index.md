## Topics Covered

### 1. Human Eye
- Structure and parts (cornea, iris, pupil, lens, retina)
- Working and image formation
- Power of accommodation
- Range of vision

### 2. Defects of Vision
- Myopia (near-sightedness) and correction
- Hypermetropia (far-sightedness) and correction
- Presbyopia and bifocal lenses
- Numerical problems on lens power

### 3. Refraction and Dispersion
- Refraction through prism
- Angle of deviation
- Dispersion of white light
- Spectrum (VIBGYOR)
- Rainbow formation

### 4. Atmospheric Phenomena
- Atmospheric refraction
- Twinkling of stars
- Advanced sunrise and delayed sunset
- Scattering of light
- Blue color of sky
- Reddish sun at sunrise/sunset

### 5. Colors
- Primary colors of light (RGB)
- Secondary colors
- Complementary colors
- Color of objects
