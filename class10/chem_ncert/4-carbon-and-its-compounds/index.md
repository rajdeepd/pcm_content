## Key Concepts

| Topic | Key Points |
|-------|------------|
| **Covalent Bond** | Sharing of electron pairs between atoms |
| **Catenation** | Ability of carbon to form bonds with itself |
| **Tetravalency** | Carbon has valency of 4 |
| **Functional Groups** | -OH, -COOH, -CHO, -CO-, -Cl |
| **Homologous Series** | Series differing by $CH_2$ unit |
