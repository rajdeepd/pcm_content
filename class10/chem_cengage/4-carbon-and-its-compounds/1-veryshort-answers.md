# Very Short Answer Type Questions

1. How does the conductivity vary in diamond and graphite?
2. What is the role of a functional group in an organic compound?
3. Aneutral organic compound ' X ' with the molecular formula $\mathrm{C}_2 \mathrm{H}_6 \mathrm{O}$, on oxidation with acidified potassium dichromate, gives ethanoic acid. What is the compound X ?
4. What is the valency of carbon in its compounds?
5. An unknown compound has the smell of vinegar. Identify it.
6. Write the molecular formula of the first two members of the homologous series having the functional group - Cl.
7. Name the functional group present in $\mathrm{CH}_3 \mathrm{COCH}_3$ and state the IUPAC name of this compound.
8. Name the process by which unsaturated fats are changed to saturated fats.
9. Write the name and molecular formula of the first member of the homologous series of alkynes.
10. The molecular formula of a hydrocarbon is $\mathrm{C}_3 \mathrm{H}_8$. Draw its complete structure and write its name.
11. Write any two oxidizing agents.

## Answers

1. How does the conductivity vary in diamond and graphite?
    - Diamond is a poor conductor of electricity, while graphite is a good conductor of electricity.
2. What is the role of a functional group in an organic compound?
    - A functional group is a group of atoms that are responsible for the chemical properties of a compound.
3. Aneutral organic compound ' X ' with the molecular formula $\mathrm{C}_2 \mathrm{H}_6 \mathrm{O}$, on oxidation with acidified potassium dichromate, gives ethanoic acid. What is the compound X ?
    - Ethanol
4. What is the valency of carbon in its compounds?
    - Carbon can form 4 covalent bonds.
5. An unknown compound has the smell of vinegar. Identify it.
    - Vinegar is acetic acid.
6. Write the molecular formula of the first two members of the homologous series having the functional group - Cl.
    - $\mathrm{CH}_3\mathrm{Cl}$ (Chloromethane) and $\mathrm{C}_2\mathrm{H}_5\mathrm{Cl}$ (Chloroethane)
7. Name the functional group present in $\mathrm{CH}_3 \mathrm{COCH}_3$ and state the IUPAC name of this compound.
    - Functional group: Ketone ($>C=O$)
    - IUPAC Name: Propanone
8. Name the process by which unsaturated fats are changed to saturated fats.
    - Hydrogenation (Addition of Hydrogen)
9. Write the name and molecular formula of the first member of the homologous series of alkynes.
    - Ethyne ($C_2H_2$)
10. The molecular formula of a hydrocarbon is $\mathrm{C}_3 \mathrm{H}_8$. Draw its complete structure and write its name.
    - Name: Propane
    - Structure:

      (Structure is $CH_3-CH_2-CH_3$)
11. Write any two oxidizing agents.
    - Alkaline Potassium Permanganate ($KMnO_4$)
    - Acidified Potassium Dichromate ($K_2Cr_2O_7$)
    - Oxygen ($O_2$)
    - Concentrated Nitric Acid ($HNO_3$)
