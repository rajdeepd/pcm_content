# <span class="section-header">Chapter 11: Electricity</span>

<div class="intro-box">
One of the most essential part of life in today's world is <b>electricity</b>. It is a controllable and convenient form of energy. It is being used in almost every sector of modern society like households, commercial, transport and industry, etc.
<br/><br/>
In this chapter, we will discuss about <b>electric current</b>, <b>electric potential</b>, <b>Ohm's law</b>, <b>resistance</b> and <b>heating effect of electric current</b>.
</div>
