# Acids, Bases and Salts

It is interesting to note that the various food items that we eat have different tastes. This is due to the fact that chemical nature of all these substances are different. On the basis of their chemical properties, the chemical compounds can be classified as acids, bases and salts. Some examples include digestive fluids of humans and animals containing acids, the bitter taste of substances like bitter gourd, cucumber etc., is due to the bases present in them, common salt is used in cooking.
