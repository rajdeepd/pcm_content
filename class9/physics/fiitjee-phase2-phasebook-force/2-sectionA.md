**Problem 1:**<br/>
A mass $m$ undergoes free fall: What is is linear momenturn after it has fallen a distance h?

Solution:<br/>
$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text { Using } v^2-u^2=2 a s \\
& \Rightarrow v=\sqrt{2 g h} \\
& \Rightarrow \text { momentum } P=m v=m \sqrt{2 g h} .
\end{aligned}
$$
